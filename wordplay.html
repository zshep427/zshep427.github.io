Wordplay is a word-based puzzle game where you need to rack up as high a score as possible before running out of plays on the board. The score of each word is the sum of the tile values in the top left corner of each tile. After playing a word, the tiles above the played word will drop and new letters will spawn in their place. The rack above the board previews the next letter that will spawn in that column.

Selecting two letters will swap their position on the board. Use them wisely, though; you have a limited number of swaps. See the tiles with red numbers in the top right corner? In order to get more swaps, play words that include red numbered tiles and whose length meet or exceed the number in the top right of the red numbered tiles.

Design, art, and engineering were all done by me. Wordplay was made using Unity and scripted entirely in C#.

Hold Left Click to select letters and play words. Words can be played up, down, left, or right.

When adding a letter surrounded by a dark blue highlight to your word, you can continue that word in any direciton.
